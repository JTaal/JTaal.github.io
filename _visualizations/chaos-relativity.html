Cosmic Entropy & Bekenstein-Hawking Formula

This bar chart visualizes cosmic entropy contributions on a log scale, showing the dominance of supermassive black holes. The entropy of a single black hole is given by the Bekenstein-Hawking formula.

$$ \frac{S_{\text{BH}}}{k_B} = \frac{4\pi G M^2}{\hbar c} $$

Advanced Bifurcation Diagram

This diagram uses a more complex "Verhulst-style" function to reveal intricate structures beyond the r=4 limit of the standard logistic map. Zoom in to explore the details.
